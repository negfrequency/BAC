The Baltimore Algorithms Club is a club devoted to collaborative philosophical problem solving.

Relatively new to Baltimore, we aim to identify unique problems, and regardless of experience work together in sharing knowledge and building software to tackle these problems, while enjoying a few brews on the way.

The 2017-2018 season will focus on the NHL's Stanley Cup by building scrapers, a database, and a model to predict the outcome of games as well as the betting spreads associated with them. Due to the small learning curve, code is written in python.

Location:   DogWatch Tavern in Fells Point, Baltimore.
Date:         Every other Wednesday, 8PM-10PM (next meeting is October 18th, 2017).
Note:           It is encouraged to bring sketch paper as opposed to a computer.
